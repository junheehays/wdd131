Chanel Classic Flap

A closer look at one of the most iconic handbags ever designed.

History & Design

The Chanel Classic Flap evolved from Coco Chanel’s original 2.55 bag. It combines a structured rectangular shape with soft quilted leather and a leather-woven chain strap.

Iconic Features

  • Diamond-quilted leather pattern
  • Leather-woven chain strap that can be worn single or doubled
  • CC turn-lock closure on the front flap
  • Back “Mona Lisa” smile pocket
