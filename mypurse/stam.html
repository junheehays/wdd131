Marc Jacobs Stam Satchel

The early-2000s it-bag that became a cult favorite.

History of the Stam

Named after model Jessica Stam, the Marc Jacobs Stam Satchel debuted in the mid-2000s and quickly became a red-carpet staple.

Design Details

  • Soft quilted leather body
  • Chunky chain shoulder strap
  • Framed top with kiss-lock closure
  • Available in multiple leathers and finishes
